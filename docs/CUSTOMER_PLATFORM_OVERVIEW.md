# Senimen Books — Customer-Facing Platform Overview

*For use in Public Offer (Terms of Service), privacy summaries, and marketing. Non-technical language.*

---

## 1. Platform purpose

**Senimen Books** is a platform for **creating personalized books**—beautiful, meaningful volumes built around your own words, your people, and your memories.

The heart of the experience is **structured storytelling**: instead of staring at a blank page, you move through a **curated path of prompts**—thoughtful questions that help you recall moments, describe relationships, and express what matters. Whether you are shaping a **life story**, a **gift for someone you love**, or a **family keepsake**, Senimen Books guides you so the book feels complete, coherent, and unmistakably *yours*.

You remain the author. The platform provides the **framework**, the **flow**, and the **place** to bring your story together—step by step—until it is ready to become something you can hold in your hands.

---

## 2. User journey & features

### The editor experience

Your book takes shape in an **interactive editor** built around **chapters and questions**. Each question invites a short or longer answer—memories, anecdotes, dedications, or reflections—so your narrative grows naturally as you go. You can work in the order that suits you, revisit earlier answers, and refine your tone until it feels right.

The experience is designed to feel **supportive, not overwhelming**: one step at a time, with your book growing richer as you add more of yourself to it.

### Multimedia integration

Words are only part of the story. You can **upload your own photos**—family snapshots, milestones, quiet everyday moments—and place them where they belong in your book. Images sit alongside your text so the final work feels like a **true personal album**, not a generic template.

### Customization

Beyond the guided questions, you can shape the book to fit **your** project. **Custom pages** let you add special sections—extra photo spreads, poems, dedications, or layouts that match how you want this book to read and feel. You decide what appears in the finished layout and what stays as a private note while you draft, so the structure reflects **your** occasion and **your** voice.

### Progress tracking

You always know **how far you have come**: your progress is visible as you move through chapters and questions. Your work is **saved automatically** as you go, and you can **close the app and come back later**—your draft waits for you, securely tied to your account, so life’s interruptions do not mean starting over.

---

## 3. Product value

What begins as a **digital draft** on Senimen Books becomes a **carefully composed layout**—pages typeset and arranged so they are ready for **professional printing** and binding. You are not handed a loose document; you are guided toward a **finished book** that honors the care you put into every answer and every image.

There is a special kind of value in turning scattered memories into **something permanent**: a gift someone will open again in ten years, a record younger family members can read when they are older, a quiet tribute on a shelf. Senimen Books exists to help you **turn what you remember into what you can keep**—a physical legacy, not just a file on a device.

---

## 4. User security & trust

**Your story belongs to you.** The words you write and the photos you upload are treated as **private content**: they are connected to **your account** and are not treated as public material. Only you (and the small team of people who help produce your book when you move to printing or review, as described in our formal terms) can access what you create in line with our policies.

We also know how much emotional weight sits in these pages. That is why the platform is built around **reliable saving**: your answers and images are **preserved as you work**, so a dropped connection or a busy day does not erase what you have already shared with the page. You can trust the process—and return whenever you are ready for the next sentence or the next chapter.
